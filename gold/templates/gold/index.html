Worth Your Weight in Gold

Welcome valued user! This interface will take your weight and convert it into troy ounces which will then be converted into an american dollar amount in an effort to answer: what would you be worth if you were made of solid gold?

The process is easy! Simply place your weight in the empty input field below, chose the relevant units of measurement from the drop-down selection menu and press "Compute". A US dollar amount will be displayed below along with the date & time that it was calculated. If you want to remove a result, then just click on it!
